Hi, I’m Miranda Marinello.

Hi I’m Miranda Marinello I can’t stop making things. I have been sewing, crocheting and knitting since I was a child. I’ve repaired poorly maintained mascot suits and helmets. As a child I soldered trailer brake lights with my dad. I work from home and spend lunch breaks curing polymer clay in the oven. I lie awake at night thinking about corset boning and thinning enamel paint for airbrushing a fiberglass motorcycle helmet from 1982. I have a kiln in my kitchen for someday, I save tutorials on 3D modeling for when I get a 3D printer. My mom told little Miranda, “You can do anything” so I did and will continue to do so. I cannot stop making things. I want to make things for you.
